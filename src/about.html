about me

personal details

background

Hello! My name is Jamilla-Rose Reynolds, but you can call me Jami. I am a high school senior and a sophomore in an early college program, working toward my Applied Associate’s degree. My journey into web design began unexpectedly in 10th grade when I took a web development class on a whim—and it quickly became a passion. Since then, I’ve been dedicated to honing my skills in web and user experience design. Beyond my studies, I take great pride in my Filipino heritage and love embracing my culture. My favorite color is pink (as you can probably tell), and I have a soft spot for anything with a strawberry aesthetic. Outside of design, I enjoy crocheting, drawing, playing games, and watching cartoons. I also share my home with two adorable dogs—a Shih Tzu and a Chihuahua. Coming from a creative family, I have three siblings, all of whom express their artistry in different ways. I am always eager to learn, create, and explore new opportunities in design and development!

passions

why I chose web development

I never expected to fall in love with web design—it all started when I took a WEB 1430 class on a whim. But as I dove into it, I realized how much I genuinely enjoyed the mix of creativity and problem-solving. There’s something really exciting about bringing ideas to life in a way that’s both beautiful and easy to use. I love designing experiences that feel natural and intuitive, making sure they’re not just visually appealing but also accessible to everyone. Through my journey, I’ve come to appreciate how thoughtful design can truly shape the way people interact with technology, and that’s what keeps me inspired every day.

education and Career path

Currently, I am advancing my skills in web and user experience design through my college coursework while working on various projects that challenge and refine my abilities. My studies have allowed me to develop a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UI/UX principles, and I am continuously expanding my knowledge through hands-on experience.

what drives me

I am passionate about creating functional and accessible digital solutions for small business owners. My inspiration comes from my mom, who has an incredible talent for cooking. She loves making food for family, friends, and anyone lucky enough to try her dishes—people always leave wanting more. Many have expressed interest in buying her food, but the challenges of running a restaurant make it difficult for her to sell it on a larger scale. Seeing this struggle firsthand motivated me to deepen my knowledge of web development. I hope that one day, I can create a platform that allows my mom—and others like her—to share their talents with a wider audience. My goal is to design and develop solutions that empower small business owners, helping them turn their passions into thriving ventures.
